        Proposal for the Establishment of an Electronic Hobbyist Club

    As we progress further through the twenty-first century, we grow more and
more reliant on technology with every passing day. Many countries, such as Ind-
ia and China, have already begun to integrate it into their curriculum. Yet,
our own United States, a country which prides itself on being at the forefront 
of the world, has not. As we speak, the gap in technology education continues 
to grow.
